Improvements on Prototype 2

After speaking with classmates, most people said that my initial prototype (above) was a bit too cluttered. Many suggested that I use a different method of visualizing the data and one method that was frequently suggested was a heatmap. Most people also liked the idea of analyzing firefighter travel times so I kept it and applied the data to a heatmap. After some trial and error, my new prototype condensed the timespan to days of the month and I ended up with the prototype below.
